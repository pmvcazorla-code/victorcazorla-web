About me

Víctor Cazorla Fernández is an executive in information technology and a researcher in natural and legal sciences. He is also an expert witness, lecturer and president of the Ethics and Deontology Committee at COAMB.

Since June 2026 I have been a member of the AQUA Pool of Experts (Agency for University Quality, Andorra), contributing to external evaluation and quality assurance in higher education.

This website is not meant to replace a conventional professional presentation or provide a curriculum summary; the what of his career can be found on LinkedIn. Here it is deliberately placed on the how: the criteria from which he interprets his practice, the conceptual frameworks that guide his decisions and the questions that shape his work as a professional, researcher and active member of the community.

You can follow my professional activity and publications on LinkedIn.
